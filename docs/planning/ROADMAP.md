# Roadmap

## Phase 1

- Define the core verification loop.
- Make the product promise instantly understandable.
- Show one complete path from AI-generated code to verified fix.
- Establish the language of trust, diagnosis, and shipping confidence.

## Phase 2

- Improve the quality of issue discovery.
- Make root cause grouping feel accurate and useful.
- Make the Codex handoff feel natural.
- Reduce the time from discovered issue to verified fix.

## Phase 3

- Support more realistic product workflows.
- Make verification results easier to review and compare.
- Introduce stronger confidence signals.
- Help developers understand what changed between verification runs.

## Phase 4

- Expand Verion into a repeatable verification layer for AI-assisted development.
- Support team-level trust and review workflows.
- Build a durable product identity around shipping confidence.
- Turn verification history into product intelligence without adding noise.
