# Product Principles

## Trust Over Hype

Verion should earn confidence through useful verification, not dramatic claims.

## Verify Before Shipping

The product exists to create a clear moment of confidence before release.

## Reduce Developer Anxiety

AI-generated code often leaves developers with uncertainty. Verion should make that uncertainty smaller.

## Explain Instead of Overwhelm

More findings are not always better. The product should explain what matters and why.

## One Opinion Is Better Than Fifty Warnings

Verion should group related symptoms and present a judgment the developer can act on.

## Autonomous, But Always Reviewable

Verion can explore and reason on its own, but the developer should always understand what happened.

## AI Should Increase Confidence, Not Uncertainty

Every AI action in Verion should help the developer trust the product more, not wonder what just happened.

## Respect Attention

The product should be concise, calm, and selective. Attention is part of the user experience.

## Make the Next Step Obvious

After every verification result, the developer should know whether to fix, verify again, or ship.
